Torched Nachos

Description

A new take on an old favorite!

Ingredients

  • Chips, any brand
  • Leftover Queso Dip
  • Cheddar Cheese Block in back of fridge, grated
  • Tomatoes, preferably homegrown

Steps

  1. Grease pan with butter.
  2. Spread out generous layer of chips.
  3. Apply liquid queso cheese liberally.
  4. Top liquid cheese with grated solid cheese and tomatoes.
  5. Place in oven on Hi broiler setting.
  6. Get distracted by computer until smoke billows out of oven and sets off fire alarm.
  7. Turn off oven and allow smoke to settle and permeate nachos.
  8. Scrape viable nachos off and enjoy!
