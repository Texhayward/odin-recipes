Texas Brisket

Description

Yeehaw!

Ingredients

  • One packer brisket
  • Lots of salt, kosher
  • Lots of pepper, Telicherry

Steps

  1. Watch Franklin BBQ youtubes and read the book.
  2. Trim brisket as per Franklin BBQ.
  3. Liberally apply salt and pepper "rub".
  4. Smoke at 250F in Traeger for 12 hours until internal temp reaches 210F. Spray with apple cider vinegar as needed.
